Introduction - The Digital Life Guard

Aqua Guardians were created for one purpose, to save precious human life from the dangers of drowning along with other emergencies in the water (i.e. a seizure).

The Idea is to outsource life guarding to Web based Guardian Teams (2-3 People) that watch over a body of water, and maneuver across them at greater speeds than a life guard could ever swim. Within each Guardian is a scouting drone, enhancing the entire teams awareness.
